Single-page hand-drawn educational journey map in a clean presentation style.

ARTICLE CONTEXT:
The article describes the climax and conclusion of effective onboarding: let users experience the core value, ask for a review at the emotional high point, summarize the user's journey, ask for commitment, show social proof, then enter the paywall.

VISUAL GOAL:
Summarize the path from value experience to paywall as a purposeful journey rather than a sudden sales page.

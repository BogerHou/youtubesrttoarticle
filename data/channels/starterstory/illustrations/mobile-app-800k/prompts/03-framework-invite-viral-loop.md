TITLE:
一条视频的增长飞轮

STRUCTURE:
Large circular loop with five rounded nodes connected by thick wavy arrows.
Place a central video card in the middle labeled:
4800 万播放
200 万点赞

1. 看视频
2. 下载 App
3. 邀请 3 个朋友
4. 评论区换邀请码
5. 互动变强

- 看视频 -> 下载 App: 好奇试妆

Add two side badges outside the loop:

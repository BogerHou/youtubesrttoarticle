Create a clean editorial sketchnote-style flowchart illustration for a Chinese business article about using the same keyword and content system for both SEO and Google Ads.

VISUAL GOAL
Visualize one integrated Google growth engine: keyword research creates useful landing pages and guides; SEO compounds over time, paid search buys immediate traffic, and both channels feed conversion data back into the system.

- 16:9 landscape canvas.
- Use a central input block: "高意图关键词".

  2. "付费搜索 Ads" lane: ad group -> custom landing page -> paid leads.
- Both lanes merge into "注册 / 付费 / 数据反馈".
- Add a feedback loop arrow returning to "优化标题、文案、CTA、关键词".
- Keep all labels short and readable.

Create a clean editorial sketchnote-style framework illustration for a Chinese business article about starting Google growth for a new SaaS from zero.

VISUAL GOAL
Turn the article's five-step playbook into a memorable operating checklist for founders.

COMPOSITION
- 16:9 landscape canvas.
- Use a left-to-right or circular five-step process.
- Each step should have a numbered card, a simple icon, and one short supporting phrase.
- Add a small dashboard strip at the bottom showing the metrics that guide decisions.
- Keep the image practical and founder-oriented.

CONTENT AND LABELS
- Title label: "Google 增长 5 步".
- Step 1: "找关键词".
- Step 2: "做内容".
- Step 3: "投广告".
- Step 4: "看数据".
- Step 5: "控成本".
- Bottom metric strip: "CAC", "ROAS", "收入", "转化率".
- Small badge: "3-6 个月".

STYLE
- Modern business sketchnote with polished editorial layout.
- Soft macaron colors, thin hand-drawn outlines, rounded cards, subtle paper texture.
- Chinese labels must be clear, short, and correctly spelled.
- No realistic human faces, no fake logos, no brand marks.
- Do not render prompt instructions or control text into the image.
- All surrounding text must remain Simplified Chinese. Proper nouns may appear only when necessary.

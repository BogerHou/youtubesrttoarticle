Create a clean editorial sketchnote-style infographic for a Chinese business article about focusing on one growth channel instead of doing ten mediocre channels.

VISUAL GOAL
Make the final strategic lesson concrete: one deeply optimized Google channel can beat many scattered channels when product, user intent, and unit economics match.

COMPOSITION
- 16:9 landscape canvas.
- Use a dashboard-style layout with one large focused channel card and one smaller "scattered channels" comparison card.
- The focused channel card should look stable and measurable, with keyword, SEO, ads, and revenue widgets.
- The scattered channels card should show many tiny weak channel icons fading out.
- Add a clear visual conclusion at the bottom.

CONTENT AND LABELS
- Main headline label: "一个渠道做到极致".
- Focus card labels: "Google 搜索", "高意图关键词", "SEO + Ads", "数据复投".
- Metrics:
  - "$40K+ MRR"
  - "SEO：$8K+ MRR"
  - "付费搜索：$15K MRR"
  - "广告花费：$8K/月"
  - "收入回报：2.5x"
  - "付费用户：700+"
- Comparison card label: "十个平庸渠道".
- Bottom conclusion: "匹配产品、意图和商业模式".

STYLE
- Modern business sketchnote infographic, not a dense financial report.
- Soft macaron colors, clean dashboard widgets, clear hierarchy, generous whitespace.
- Chinese labels and numbers must be clear, short, and correctly spelled.
- No realistic human faces, no fake logos, no brand marks.
- Do not render prompt instructions or control text into the image.
- All surrounding text must remain Simplified Chinese. Proper nouns may appear only when necessary.
